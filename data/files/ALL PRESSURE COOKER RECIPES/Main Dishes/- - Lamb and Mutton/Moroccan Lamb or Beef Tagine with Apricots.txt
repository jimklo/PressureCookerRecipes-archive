Curried Lamb Chops - 4 qt cooker

INGREDIENTS

4 shoulder lamb chops about 4 ounces each
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 medium onions diced
2 small apples diced
2 teaspoons curry powder
1 cup chicken bouillon
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon pepper


Trim chops of excess fat.
Heat oil in cooker then brown chops on both sides.
Remove chops then add onions and apples.
Sprinkle curry powder over the onions and apples then lightly cook
until  onions are soft.
Add bouillon, cinnamon, and pepper and stir.
Put chops back into cooker spooning sauce over chops.
Cook at high pressure for 6 minutes and then allow natural pressure
release.

NOTE: Don't like lamb? Try with pork chops but cook a little longer if
using pork. May also be used with beef cubes and cooked for 7 minutes
and allow for natural pressure release.
