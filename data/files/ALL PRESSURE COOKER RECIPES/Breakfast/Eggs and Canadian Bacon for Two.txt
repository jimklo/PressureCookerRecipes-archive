Old Fashioned Steel-Cut Oats

4 - 6 Servings

INGREDIENTS

2 1/2 cups water or apple cider
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup steel-cut oats, or pinhead oats
1 cinnamon stick

METHOD

1 Place all ingredients in cooking pot.

2 Lock lid in place.

3 Select Rice function, and set timer for 12 minutes.

4 When cooking is complete, and all pressure is released, remove lid.
Serve hot with milk or cream, if desired.

Note: These oats can be sweetened with honey or maple syrup and
dried fruit. For an additional health benefit, add a tablespoon of 
flax seed before serving.
