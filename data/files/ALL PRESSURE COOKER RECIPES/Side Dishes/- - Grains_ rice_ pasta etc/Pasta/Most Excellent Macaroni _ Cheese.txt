Most Excellent Macaroni & Cheese

Recipe By :Bob Warden - QVC
Serving Size : 6 Preparation Time :0:00
Categories : Kids Pressure Cooker

Amount Measure Ingredient -- Preparation Method
-------- ------------ --------------------------------
Macaroni:

2 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
2 teaspoons chicken bouillon granules -- mixed into 2 cups of water
1 cup water
2 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese

Cheese:

2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
2 ounces cream cheese
1 teaspoon yellow mustard

Add all the macaroni ingredients to pressure cooker, set cooker on high and cook for 6 minutes.

Do quick release. Remove lid and slowly stir in cheese ingredients until melted and creamy.
